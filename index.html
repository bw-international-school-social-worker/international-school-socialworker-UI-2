Our Mission

To solve educational challenges in remote and low resource environments by providing a way for school administrators and social workers to help their students succeed.

What is Educell?

Educell is an educational tool that can be used to evaluate the needs of their students, identify underserved individuals, and share their data with social worker, school administrators, and other service providers.

This app will allow school anministrators and social works to be organized in a way that would not have been possible otherwise.

Empowering Educators

Educell provides resources for educators globally. Educators can use the data gathered on their students, and implement different teaching techniques to meet the needs of the students.

We have created this app to provides a way for school administrators and social workers to create individual student profiles.
